# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
the Kyvik community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

We expect everyone to:

- Be respectful, considerate, and collaborative.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

Behavior that makes others feel unwelcome or unsafe is not tolerated.

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its full code
of conduct. Please read it here:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported confidentially to the
project maintainer at **kyvik@kkjorsvik.com**. All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the
privacy and security of anyone who reports an incident.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
